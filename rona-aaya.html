Rona Aaya

✍️ SPureRuh aka C. Shekhar
🗓️ 26 January 2025, 07:41:40

Phir, na jaane kyon kis baat par mujhe rona aaya  
Khud ki baatein khud se karke khud par hi rona aaya  
Kya wajood mera, kyun laya mujhe yahaan par, kya galti meri  
Lakar mujhe yahaan, galti meri, bataa kar, mere wajood ko  
Mujh par rona aaya  
Gairon ka gila nahi mujhe, mila apnon se yeh tohfa  
Shabab dil dukhayi ka  
Apni hi baat sunkar, apni hi dil lagayi par rona aaya  
Mila samandar nadiyon ko  
Mila aasman sooraj ko  
Samaa ke khud ko  
Jala ke khud ko  
Khud hi ko mitane par rona aaya  
Mit’ta ja raha hoon, jalta ja raha hoon  
Mitkar, jalkar aayi labon ki  
Is hansi par rona aaya  
Phir, na jaane kyon kis baat par mujhe, phir rona aaya।
